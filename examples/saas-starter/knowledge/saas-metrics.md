# SaaS Metrics & Benchmarks

> Reference benchmarks for AI agents working on SaaS products.
> Use these to evaluate your product's health and set targets.

---

## Key SaaS Metrics

### Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue | Growing 10-20% MoM (early) |
| **ARR** | Annual Recurring Revenue (MRR × 12) | $1M+ for Series A |
| **ARPU** | Average Revenue Per User | Depends on segment |
| **NRR** | Net Revenue Retention (expansion - churn) | >100% (ideally 120%+) |
| **GRR** | Gross Revenue Retention (before expansion) | >85% |

### Growth Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **MoM Growth** | Month-over-month revenue growth | 15-20% (early stage) |
| **T2D3** | Triple twice, double thrice (YoY pattern) | $1M → $3M → $9M → $18M → $36M → $72M |
| **Payback Period** | Time to recoup CAC | <12 months |
| **Rule of 40** | Growth rate + profit margin | >40% combined |

### Unit Economics

| Metric | Definition | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost | <⅓ of LTV |
| **LTV** | Lifetime Value | >3x CAC |
| **LTV:CAC** | Ratio of lifetime value to acquisition cost | 3:1 to 5:1 |
| **Churn Rate** | % customers leaving per month | <2% monthly |
| **Logo Churn** | Customer count churn | <5% annually |

### Sales Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **Win Rate** | % of opportunities closed won | 20-30% |
| **Sales Cycle** | Average days from first touch to close | Varies by ACV |
| **Pipeline Coverage** | Pipeline value / quota | 3-4x |
| **Speed to Lead** | Time from inquiry to first response | <5 minutes |
| **Demo-to-Close** | % of demos that convert | 25-40% |

## ACV-Based Benchmarks

| ACV Range | Typical Sales Cycle | Sales Motion | Team Structure |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------|----------------|
| <$1K | Self-serve | Product-led | No sales team needed |
| $1K-$10K | 14-30 days | Inside sales | SDR + AE |
| $10K-$50K | 30-90 days | Mid-market | AE + SE |
| $50K-$250K | 90-180 days | Enterprise | AE + SE + CSM |
| >$250K | 180-365 days | Strategic | Named accounts team |
