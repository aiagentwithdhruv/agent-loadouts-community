## Pricing Strategies

### Value-Based Pricing
Calculate based on client ROI, not your hours:
- If automation saves client 20 hrs/week × $50/hr = $52K/year savings
- Price at 10-20% of annual savings: $5K-10K

### Retainer Model
- Monthly retainer for ongoing support
- Typical: $1,000-5,000/month
- Includes: X hours support + priority response + monitoring

### Productized Services
- Fixed-scope, fixed-price packages
- Example: "AI Chatbot Setup Package — $2,500"
- Removes pricing uncertainty for both sides
